Fabrication process

The model was built in stages so the complex B-2 geometry could be handled more carefully and finished more cleanly.

PLA core preparation

The 3D-printed PLA sections were joined using steel-infused glue and heavily sanded to remove polygon-like print geometry before carbon fiber layup.

That prep work mattered because print lines and sharp facets would otherwise telegraph through the composite surface later.
