Carbon fiber B-2 Spirit model for the Mechanical Vibrations Lab

This project upgraded the older 1-foot B-2 model into a larger 3.5-foot composite model using a 3D-printed PLA core, wet carbon fiber layup, vacuum bagging, finishing, repair work, and continued spare-model production.

View fabrication Download memo

Project overview

Scale upgrade

Replaced the earlier 1-foot model with a 3.5-foot version close to 1:50 scale.

Composite construction

Built using a 3D-printed PLA core with carbon fiber wet layup and vacuum bagging.

Continued lab support

Three models were completed in Summer 2024, and additional spare models are being produced.
